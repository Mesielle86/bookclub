WELCOME TO EAST LONDON BOOK CLUB

Read

In our East London Book Club anybody welcome who is over 18 of age and have the interest in self-help books or would like to strengthen their reading habit. We vote for a book monthly to read and discuss on our next meeting. On our gatherings we discuss about the chosen book, share ideas and reflect on the topic. We talk about examples on the topic in our real life. Every month a new book to understand yourself and others exploring different topics completely free.
