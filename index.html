Read

In our East London Book Club anybody welcome who is over 18 of age and have the interest in self-help books or would like to strengthen their reading habit. We vote for a book monthly to read and discuss on our meeting.

Learn

On our monthly gathering we discuss about the book that was voted for, share ideas and reflect on the topic. We talk about examples on the topic in our real life.

Repeat

Every month a new book to understand yourself and others exploring different topics.
